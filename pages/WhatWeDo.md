The following is a short list of the technical areas in which we provide training, mentoring, and consulting services to our customers:
## Statistics and Quality Engineering
* General statistical and graphical data analysis
* Process capability studies
* Operational and performance qualification
* Gage error studies
* Analytical method validation
* Design of experiments (DOE)
* Statistical process control (SPC)
* Short-run SPC
* Acceptance sampling (to accepted standards and custom designs)
* Six Sigma (GB, BB, MBB)
* Quality management systems (ISO, QS, ...)
* Custom software programming and support
* Excel programming
* MINITAB programming
* Parametric and nonparametric statistical methods
* ISO17025 implementation, maintenance, etc.
* Dimensional metrology
* Calibration systems management
* Measurement uncertainty
* Reliability
* Technical reports
* Sample size and power calculations
* SBIR grant support
